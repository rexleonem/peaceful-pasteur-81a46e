We are Black Village

We are a creative video production company based in Lagos, Nigeria and serving Africa and the world. We are a talented team of directors, producers, editors and film crew that specialize in producing professional and high quality music videos, TV commercials, movies, event coverage and other video related services.

We aim to produce crestive and innovative Video contents by networking through collaboration and co-productions with writers, directors, producers and other production houses, either new or established.

What We Do


We Offer Creative Solutions

Film Making

We provide wide range of film and video production support services for film and video production in different Locations. We are capable of finding suitable location to your specification. We can take care of your needs and ensure you will get the best experience and widest exposure to the resources.

DOP Services

The director of photography (also known as the DP or cinematographer) is hired at the start of preproduction, and is typically hand-picked by the director. This person will supervise the camera, lighting, and grip departments, and is tasked with selecting the cameras, lenses, filters, film, and other accessories required for principal photography.

Photography

A picture is worth a thousand words as they say, it’s crucial to entrust your photography needs with us as we can help tell your story the right way. At Black Village, we give your brand a memorable visual identity through corporate photography, industrial photography, event photography, and more.

Music Video Production

Each song tells a story and we help that story come alive ensuring that your audience get to see the imagery behind the music.

Documentary

We love finding meaningful stories in the reality that surrounds us, and discovering the best language to tell that story. We visualise real stories & cinematic narratives that speak of social issues.

Video and film Editing

We will turn your video clips and personal recordings into full phase Hi-quality films, quickly and effortlessly through our video editing service. You have to just send the video to us and our professional video editor will take care from here. We edit, organize, fix, add effects, caption, background music and whatever else you like. We will handle all the works for you.

Event Coverage and Planning
